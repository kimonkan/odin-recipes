Spotchka

Description

Spotchka was a beverage that could be created from the creatures known as
krill. It could be found on Sorgan, brewed by the resident farmers of an
isolated farming village. Klatooinian raiders who often attacked the
village stole the krill from the farmers, brewing their own spotchka.

DISCLAIMER: No krills were harmed.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Blue Curacao
  • 1/3 oz Lime Juice
  • 3 oz Club Soda
  • splash of Simple Syrup

Steps

  1. Add gin, blue curacao, lime juice and a splash of syrup in a shaker.
  2. Shake vigorously over ice.
  3. Strain into the glass.
  4. Top with club soda to taste.
