Blue Milk

Description

Blue milk, also known as Bantha milk, was a rich blue-colored milk produced by female banthas. Sentients drank it, and also used it in bantha butter, blue bantha buttermilk biscuits, blue-milk cheese, blue milk custard, ice cream, and yogurt. It was notably available on Outer Rim planets such as Batuu, Lah'mu, Lothal, and Tatooine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup passion fruite juice
  • 3 tbsp coconut syrup
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp melon syrup
  • 1 blue food coloring

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in the jar of a blender.
  2. Add a couple drops of blue food coloring.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. If needed, add a bit more coloring.
  5. Divide between glasses, then top with whipped cream and a cherry for a special treat!
