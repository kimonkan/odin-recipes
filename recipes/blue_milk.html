Blue Milk

Description

Blue milk, also known as Bantha milk, was a rich blue-colored milk
produced by female banthas. Sentients drank it, and also used it in
bantha butter, blue bantha buttermilk biscuits, blue-milk cheese,
blue milk custard, ice cream, and yogurt. It was notably available
on Outer Rim planets such as Batuu, Lah'mu, Lothal, and Tatooine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1 cup Pineapple Juice
  • 1/2 cup Passion Fruite Juice
  • 3 tbsp Coconut Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Lime Juice
  • 2 tbsp Melon Syrup
  • 1 Blue Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in the jar of a blender.
  2. Add a couple drops of blue food coloring.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. If needed, add a bit more coloring.
  5. Divide between glasses, then top with whipped cream and a cherry for a special treat!
