T-16 Skyhopper

Description

The T-16 Skyhopper was a mixed alcoholic beverage served at Oga's
Cantina in Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu in 34 ABY.
The yellow-colored cocktail was suitable for human consumption.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Half and Half
  • 4 oz Monin Kiwi Syrup
  • 1 oz Tito's Handmade Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Bols Melon Liqueur
  • 1 slice Kiwiskin removed, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in a shaker.
  2. Shake or stir.
  3. Serve on ice.
  4. GArnish the glass with a slice of kiwi.
